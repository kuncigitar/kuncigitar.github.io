15. i didnt go / i dont go


Keduanya bisa digunakan. Namun, tergantung waktu. Jika peristiwa tersebut terjadi di masa lampau, maka digunakanlah "I didnt go."
sedangkan, jika digunakan untuk setiap hari, atau setiap waktu tersebut, maka digunakan "I don't go."

Contoh:
"I didn't go to school yesterday." -> yesterday merupakan kata keterangan yang lazimnya digunakan untuk hal formal.

"I don't go to school every sunday." -> every sunday merupakan kata keterangan. Yang mana hal tersebut dilakukan setiap hari minggu.i didn't go = utk past tense
i don't go = utk present tense

35. Prabu: "Ratu, are you going to let me go without you?" It means ... A. Prabu asked whether Ratu is going to let him go without her me B. Prabu asked Ratu to let him go without me C. Prabu told Ratu that she was going to let him go without her D. Prabu asked Ratu if she was going to let him go without her E. Prabu asked if she was going to let Ratu go without him


Prabu: "Ratu, are you going to let me go without you?"

It means Prabu asked Ratu if she was going to let him go without her (D).

Pembahasan

Soal di atas adalah mengubah kalimat langsung (direct sentence) menjadi kalimat tidak langsung (indirect sentence).

Pada kalimat langsung yang menggunakan interrogative question (yes/no question), menggunakan if atau whether. Dua-duanya dapat digunakan.

Yang perlu diperhatikan adalah pada indirect sentence menggunakan bentuk lampau. Pada soal mengunakn present continuous tense, sehingga indirect speech-nya adalah past continuous tense (was/were + going to).
