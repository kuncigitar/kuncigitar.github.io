6. meaning of songs : when I was your man, firework dlm bhs inggris minimal 5 kalimat


When I Was Your Man:
The song tells us about a guy (Bruno Mars) who just broke up with a girl. The reason why they broke up was because he didn't treat the girl right. Now he realizes that he just lost an amazing girl and he regrets it. Sadly, that girl already has a new boyfriend. He hopes that her new boyfriend will treat her right.

Firework:
Katy Perry sings to every person out there who doesn't feel good about themselves. She tells them to show their light and color like a firework. She tells them to show their true self and no need to be afraid of that. If they will ever think that they're a waste in this world, she tells them to carry on. She also tells them to not worry about being fail because that means something good will happen. :)
